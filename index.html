How we keep you safe

Swap Across is built around trust, community, and clear boundaries

Public venues only

All first meetups take place in a verified public coffee shop, never a private address.

Reviewed profiles

Every profile is checked by the Swap Across team before it goes live.

Community code of conduct

All members agree to our guidelines before joining. We take any breach seriously.

Easy reporting

You're in good hands

Swap Across is built around care and respect. Here's what every member — and our team — commits to.

  • Your first meetup will always be in a public coffee shop — we suggest the venue so you never have to worry about where to go.
  • Every profile is reviewed by our team before you can connect — so you know who you're meeting.
  • Everyone here has agreed to treat each other with patience and kindness — whatever their age or experience.
  • If anything ever feels wrong, you can report it from any profile and we'll respond within 24 hours.
